Mission

The Diggers Beach Volleyball website is intended to improve the Diggers Beach Volleyball business by advertising their service to attract customers

Success Evaluation

There will be a monthly count which compare the number of visitor with the number of new member who sign up. Furthermore, the number of people playing at diggers each week will also be counted. The site can be call a success if the the number of new member are above a certain percentage to web visitor and the number of people playing at diggers each week increases.

Target Audience

Younger audience aged around 15 to 25, mostly high school kids and young adults.
